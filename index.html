What is MaracatuTech?

MaracatuTech is a unique conference that bridges the world of cutting-edge technology with the rich, ancestral rhythms of Maracatu. We believe that innovation and tradition are not opposing forces but two sides of the same coin.

Our mission is to create a space where developers, artists, thinkers, and creators can come together to explore how ancient cultural practices can inspire modern technological solutions. From AI-generated music that respects traditional patterns to blockchain for preserving cultural heritage, we are at the forefront of a new digital renaissance.
